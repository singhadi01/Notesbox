Welcome to Notesbox

Notesbox helps you organize and save your learning resources efficiently.

How it Helps You

  • Save and organize YouTube video links by subjects.
  • Access all your learning resources in one place.
  • Easy and quick search for your saved videos.
  • We also share the playlist we made by the code showing beside the subject name.
  • We have a section to enter the subject code for quick access.
  • Use the Solve Doubt feature to get answers to all your questions instantly.

How to Use

  1. Sign up or log in to your account.
  2. Add video links categorized by subject.
  3. View and manage your saved resources from the homepage.
  4. Use the subject code to quickly share playlists.
  5. Click on the Solve Doubt button in the navigation bar to get answers to all your questions.
